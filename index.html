Rules FAQ

What's the tournament format?

Single round robin preliminaries, then seeded Quarter-Finals, Semi-Finals, Battle for Bronze, and the Championship. Rankings start with total wins, then set ratio, points ratio, and head-to-head.

How much is the entry fee and what does it include?

₱3,500 per person, covering 2 jerseys, court fees for guaranteed games, raffle prizes, medals, staff, documentation, and promotion.

When and where are the games?

August 29, 30, 31, September 5, and September 6 at Gameville Ball Park. Teams should be at the venue 30 minutes before their scheduled match.

How do matches work?

Most matches are best of 3 sets: sets 1 and 2 to 25, deciding set to 15, always win by two. The Championship is best of 5.

What happens if we're late or short on players?

There is a 5-minute grace period from scheduled start. Fielding 5 players or fewer is an automatic default.
